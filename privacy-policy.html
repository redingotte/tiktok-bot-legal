Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the “compte2” application ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you authorize it to connect to your TikTok account.

1. Information We Access

When you authorize this application via TikTok's OAuth flow, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant, including:

  • Basic profile information (e.g., open_id, display name, avatar) via user.info.basic to identify the authorized account.
  • Authorization data (access/refresh tokens) and permissions required to upload and/or publish videos via the Content Posting API.

We do not collect additional personal information beyond what is needed to operate the integration, and we do not share your data with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use basic profile information only to confirm which TikTok account has been authorized. We use the access token solely to upload and/or publish video content to your TikTok account as authorized.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Access tokens are stored locally on your own device or server under your control, only as needed to operate the integration and publish content. We do not maintain a central database of user credentials or personal information.

Tokens are retained until you revoke access in your TikTok account settings, or until you request deletion (see “Data Deletion” below).

4. Data Deletion

To request deletion of data related to your authorization (e.g., tokens and technical identifiers used to operate the integration), contact us at compte1.meta@google.com with your TikTok username and the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Access tokens are stored securely and are not transmitted to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: compte1.meta@google.com
