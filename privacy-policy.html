Last updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you authorize it to connect to your TikTok account.

When you authorize this application via TikTok's OAuth flow, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant, limited to content publishing on your behalf. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the access token granted by you solely to publish video content to your TikTok account as authorized. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties.

3. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally on your own device or server. We do not maintain a central database of user credentials or personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

This application uses the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Access tokens are stored securely and never transmitted to third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
